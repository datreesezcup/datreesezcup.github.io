Install Instructions

  1. Click Download on the desired application
  2. You may get a warning saying:
    • This type of file can harm your device. Do you want to keep <appname> anyway?
    • Click OK
  3. Once downloaded, open your notification panel and click the download notification for the application.
  4. You should be presented with a screen asking if you would like to install the app. Click install.
  5. (Optional, but recommended) Click the button labeled Open to read details about the application.

That's it! The application is now installed on your device, and all that's left is to enable them

For Splat2 Widgets:

Open your widget list and find the entry labeled Splat2 Widgets


For Splat2 Edge:

Please note that not all Samsung devices with an Edge are compatible with Splat2 Edge. The best way to check if your device is compatible is to launch Splat2 Edge from your app drawer; you will be told whether or not your device is compatible.

If your device is compatible then navigate to your Edge Settings and enable the panels you wish to be added to your Edge.
