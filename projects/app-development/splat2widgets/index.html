About

Splat2Widgets is an app that provides 4 widgets for users to put on their home screens to give them current game information for the game Splatoon 2. The app obtains its data from the fan made site https://splatoon2.ink. The creator of the site: Matt Isenhower, has kindly enabled developers to use the data his site gathers in their own projects.
