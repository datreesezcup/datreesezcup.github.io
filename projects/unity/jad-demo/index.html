Overview

In this showcase, you can control 3 military vehicles around a small city

Global Controls

  • Cycle to next vehicle: Tab
  • Cycle to next camera view: C

JADVU Controls

  • Fly forwards/left/backwards/right: W/A/S/D
  • Rotate left/right: Q/E
  • Increase/Decrease Elevation: +/-

JADTU Controls

  • Walk forwards/backwards: W/S
  • Rotate|Strafe left/right: A/D
  • Rotate turret left/right: ←/→
  • Aim barrels up/down: ↑/↓
  • Switch between tank/walker mode: E

JADWU Controls

  • Walk forwards/backwards: W / S
  • Rotate left/right: A/D

Controls

  • Move to top position: W or ↑
  • Move to left position: A or ←
  • Move to right position: D or →
  • Move to bottom position: S or ↓
